Meet the Fight
Leadership Team

Fight is a stable WoW Guild and community that has existed for over 25 years. Starting in Ultima Online Beta, and Dark Age of Camelot we eventually founded a home in Warcraft on Day One in Vanilla WoW, and we've picked up long term guildies in almost every expansion and game since. We're a close knit group who try to run a lot together, (Mythic+ Dungeons, Raids, BGs etc.) This is a stable long term home.We are a Guild built on discipline, loyalty, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Every leader on this page has bled for this Guild and earned their place. Together, we develop fighters — and leaders — who carry this Guild's standard wherever they go.
